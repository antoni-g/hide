Welcome to a new way to play Hide and Seek! Choose whether you are going to be a hider or seeker. Hiders are able to locate other hiders as black dots on a radar, and seekers are blue dots. Since the radar refreshes only every few seconds, be sure to move quickly! Seekers are able to locate the closest hider with a distance meter. They don't have a radar! Once a hider is caught, simply leave the webapp.
